Meet Naveen – Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking Trainer

Certified Ethical Hacking Professional

I'm Naveen, a Certified Ethical Hacking (CEH) professional and cybersecurity trainer with a strong command over offensive security, vulnerability assessment, and penetration testing.

My goal is to empower aspiring cybersecurity professionals with the skills and mindset needed to ethically defend and protect in an ever-evolving digital world.
